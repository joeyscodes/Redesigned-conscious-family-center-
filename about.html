About Conscious Family Centre

A place where children, parents, and caregivers feel connected, supported, and inspired.

Conscious Family Centre is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja designed for children ages 0 to 10.

Who we are

A modern family-centered learning environment with heart.

The Conscious Family Centre exists to create a better kind of educational and community experience for young children and the adults who care for them. It blends nurturing play, creative exploration, family support, and conscious development into one warm, welcoming environment.

Rather than feeling formal or rigid, the experience is designed to feel alive, grounded, relational, and deeply human.
