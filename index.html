🌿 About Us

More Than a Playgroup.
A Community.

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja designed for children ages 0 to 10.

We offer stay-and-play sessions, a homeschool hub, and community support for parents and caregivers — all within a thoughtful, warm, and intentional environment that honours the child's natural way of learning.

🕙

Opening Hours

Monday – Saturday  |  10:00 AM – 3:00 PM

🌱

Our Mission

To create a safe, joy-filled, and intentional space where children aged 0–10 discover themselves through nature, creativity, and conscious play — while empowering families with the community and tools they need to thrive.

🔭

Our Vision

To be Abuja's leading nature-inspired family learning hub — pioneering a new standard of holistic, conscious early childhood education that nurtures the whole child: mind, body, and spirit.

Programs Built for Little Explorers

Every child is unique. Every program is crafted with love, intention, and research-backed methodology.

✦ Core Programs
🏡

Stay & Play Sessions

Drop in with your little one for structured, nature-inspired free play. Perfect for children 0–5 and caregivers who want guided playtime in a safe, stimulating environment.

  • Ages 0 – 5
  • Drop-in friendly
  • Guided by educators
📚

Homeschool Hub & Community

A dedicated co-learning space for homeschooling families — with curriculum support, peer socialisation, parent circles, and expert-led enrichment sessions.

  • Ages 4 – 10
  • Curriculum aligned
  • Parent community
👩‍🏫

Nanny Intensive Training

Professional development for caregivers covering child development, age-appropriate play, nutrition basics, emotional intelligence, and conscious caregiving techniques.

  • Certificate awarded
  • Practical skills
  • Expert trainers
🚀

Holiday Camps & S.T.E.A.M

Action-packed holiday programmes featuring Scavenger Hunts, Messy Play, Science experiments, Technology discovery, Engineering challenges, Art, and Math adventures.

  • Ages 3 – 10
  • Holiday periods
  • Hands-on STEAM

❓ Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you visit us for the first time.

CFC warmly welcomes children aged 0 to 10 years. Our programmes are carefully designed to match different developmental stages — from babies and toddlers in Stay & Play, to older children in the Homeschool Hub and STEAM camps.

For Stay & Play sessions, drop-ins are welcome! For Homeschool Hub, Nanny Training, and Holiday Camps, we do recommend contacting us in advance to confirm availability and secure a spot.

CFC is not a daycare or conventional school. We are a conscious, nature-connected learning community. Our philosophy is rooted in child-led play, forest schooling, emotional intelligence, creative expression, and community — values that are rarely found in traditional childcare settings in Abuja.

Absolutely! Many of our sessions are designed as stay-and-play experiences where parents and caregivers are encouraged to participate. We believe in whole-family engagement and building a strong parent community.

"Waka" means "to walk/go" in Nigerian Pidgin. Waka Wednesday is our beloved weekly excursion day where children venture out into Abuja — visiting museums, parks, farms, cultural spaces, and community places to expand their worldview and build confidence in real-world settings.

Not at all! While we have a dedicated Homeschool Hub, CFC is open to all families — whether your children attend conventional school, are homeschooled, or are still too young for school. We are a community space for everyone who values conscious, nature-connected childhood.

Comfortable, play-friendly clothing is best — we get messy, muddy, and creative! Closed shoes are recommended for outdoor sessions. For Stay & Play, you can bring snacks and a change of clothes. We provide all art and craft materials.

The easiest way is to call or WhatsApp us on 08035183784, or send us a DM on Instagram @consciousfamilycentre. You can also simply visit us during opening hours (Mon–Sat, 10AM–3PM) at BMT Garden, Wuse 2, Abuja — we'd love to show you around!
